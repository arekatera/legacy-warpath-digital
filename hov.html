Big Ferg L.H

The left hemisphere of the brain and right ventricle of the heart that is Warpath. He is the OG visionary behind Warpath, which was an idea born from his passion for fostering an inclusive, hardcore-driven music culture. Big Ferg L.H specialises in the deep, dark, and militant sounds of Jungle and Drum & Bass, regularly spinning tracks from legends like Rumbleton and Friske. Ferg's sets are intense voyages that fill you with the vigor needed to head into battle. On top of this, he is also a prominent member of Aotearoa's HC scene; having roots in bands such as Severed Beliefs. Ferg also has a commitment to putting on all-ages shows, stemming from his own introduction to gigging, dedicating numerous hours to ensuring the next generation has the same opportunities to experience live music in its rawest form.
